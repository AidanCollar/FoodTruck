Who are we?

"The Screamers" isn't just an ice cream truck; it's a mobile carnival of joy, bringing smiles and laughter wherever it goes. Whether you're a child delighting in your first taste of ice cream or an adult indulging in a nostalgic treat, "The Screamers" promises an experience that's as unforgettable as it is delicious.

Our Services

The truck's menu boasts an array of frozen delights, from classic cones to innovative creations that push the boundaries of imagination. Imagine towering sundaes crowned with a riot of toppings, banana splits dripping with decadent sauces, and hand-spun milkshakes in every flavor imaginable. Each treat promises not just sweetness but also a touch of whimsy and nostalgia. We have a catering service for a number of events including birthdays, open houses, or even wedding receptions

Why choose us?

Choosing "The Screamers" ice cream truck isn't just about satisfying your sweet tooth—it's about embracing an unforgettable experience that goes beyond mere dessert. Here are a few reasons why "The Screamers" stands out:

  1. Unique Flavors and Creations: We don't just offer your typical ice cream cones. "The Screamers" boasts a menu filled with innovative flavors and creative concoctions that you won't find anywhere else. From exotic fruit sorbets to indulgent ice cream sandwiches with unexpected fillings, every treat is a delightful surprise for your taste buds.
  2. Interactive Entertainment: Our ice cream truck isn't just a vehicle for delivering frozen treats—it's a mobile party on wheels! Our friendly staff goes above and beyond to entertain customers with costumes, music, and even spontaneous dance performances. When you choose "The Screamers," you're not just buying ice cream; you're joining in on the fun.
  3. Nostalgic Charm: Remember the excitement of hearing the ice cream truck's jingle as a child? "The Screamers" brings back those fond memories with its whimsical design and classic tunes. It's a nostalgic trip down memory lane for adults and a magical experience for kids.
