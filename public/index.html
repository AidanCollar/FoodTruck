Who are we?

"The Screamers" isn't just an ice cream truck; it's a mobile carnival of joy, bringing smiles and laughter wherever it goes. Whether you're a child delighting in your first taste of ice cream or an adult indulging in a nostalgic treat, "The Screamers" promises an experience that's as unforgettable as it is delicious.

Our Services

The truck's menu boasts an array of frozen delights, from classic cones to innovative creations that push the boundaries of imagination. Imagine towering sundaes crowned with a riot of toppings, banana splits dripping with decadent sauces, and hand-spun milkshakes in every flavor imaginable. Each treat promises not just sweetness but also a touch of whimsy and nostalgia. We have a catering service for a number of events including birthdays, open houses, or even wedding receptions
